## 🚀 Featured Project — Lumio

> *Your Career, Illuminated.*

**The problem:** Job seekers waste hours manually scrolling job boards to figure out what skills employers want.

**The solution:** Lumio analyzes the market for you — then helps you close your skill gaps and build a tailored resume.

```
Search Job Title → Market Intelligence → Skill Gap Analysis → AI Resume Builder
```

**Unlike competitors that start from your resume, Lumio starts from real market data.**
